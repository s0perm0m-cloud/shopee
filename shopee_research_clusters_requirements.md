# Shopee Design Research & Requirements

## Cluster 0: Product Discovery & Trust
**Objective:** Introduce authenticity badges and verified seller indicators on search results; refine ranking algorithms to reduce duplicate noise.
**Pain Point:** Users struggle to find the right products amid too many similar listings and have concerns about authenticity.
**Persona:** Working full-time (8am-5pm).
**Brand Tone:** Calm, trustworthy, focused, minimal.

## Cluster 1: Search & Filter Friction
**Objective:** Improve search relevance by distinguishing core products from accessories; surface smarter filter defaults.
**Pain Point:** Users encounter friction from irrelevant results and search "noise".
**Persona:** 40yo Homemaker.

## Cluster 2: Usage Frequency & Retention
**Objective:** Introduce personalised weekly deals, 'back again?' shortcuts, and usage-based loyalty rewards.
**Pain Point:** Users are regular but could be more deeply engaged.
**Persona:** 25yo part-time student.

## Cluster 3: Value & Comparison Gap
**Objective:** Double down on promotion visibility; introduce a side-by-side product compare feature.
**Pain Point:** Comparing shortlisted items remains difficult despite low prices and easy checkout.
**Persona:** 50yo.

---
**Shared Constraints:**
- Platform: Mobile (iOS + Android)
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.2 AA
- Requirements: Primary action button, descriptive empty state, non-modal error handling.
- Cognitive Science: 2-tap resolution (Fitts's Law), Max 5 choices per screen (Miller's Law), Progressive disclosure (Hick's Law).
